EPP

The First Data Equipment Protection Program provides FULL REPLACEMENT protection for loss due to a wide variety of perils.

  • Accidental Damage
  • Burglary Theft and Robbery
  • Collapse
  • Employee Theft
  • Falling Objects
  • Fire and Smoke
  • Hail
  • Flood and Water
  • Lightning and Power Surge
  • Transit
  • Vandalism
  • Wind, Hurricane and Tornado
Perils shown in bold above are often not covered by standard business insurance policies.
