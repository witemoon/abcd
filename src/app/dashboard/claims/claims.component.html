How to Raise a Claim


If you have experienced a loss due to one of the following reasons:

  • Accidental Damage
  • Collapse
  • Falling Objects
  • Hail Flood and Water
  • Transit
  • Wind, Hurricane and Tornado
  • Burglary theft and Robbery
  • Employee theft
  • Fire and Smoke
  • Lightning and Power Surge
  • Vandalism



Please follow these steps:

1. Email FDGLClientServices@firstdata.com (Include the reason for the loss)

2. First Data will review your claim

3. First Data will request a device to be mailed to you

4. Equipment will reach you within 24-48 hours once the claim is raised
